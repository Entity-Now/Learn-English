# 助动词 / 系动词与时态、人称关系学习手册

## 1. 概念说明
- **助动词（Auxiliary Verbs）**：用于帮助构成各种时态、语态和语气，常见的有 **do, does, did, have, has, had, will, shall**。
- **系动词（Linking Verbs）**：用于连接主语和表语，表示状态或特征，最常见的是 **be动词（am, is, are, was, were）**。

---

## 2. 助动词分类及作用
| 助动词 | 作用 |
|--------|------|
| do / does / did | 构成一般疑问句、否定句、强调句 |
| have / has / had | 构成完成时态 |
| will / shall | 构成将来时态 |
| be (am, is, are, was, were) | 构成进行时态、被动语态 |

---

## 3. 系动词与时态、人称对应关系

### **一般现在时**
| 人称 | 系动词 | 助动词 |
|------|--------|--------|
| I    | am     | do     |
| You  | are    | do     |
| He/She/It | is | does   |
| We    | are   | do     |
| They  | are   | do     |

### **一般过去时**
| 人称 | 系动词 | 助动词 |
|------|--------|--------|
| I    | was    | did    |
| You  | were   | did    |
| He/She/It | was | did  |
| We    | were  | did    |
| They  | were  | did    |

### **一般将来时**
| 人称 | 系动词 | 助动词 |
|------|--------|--------|
| 所有人称 | will be | will |

---

## 4. 时态与助动词对应关系

| 时态 | 助动词结构 | 示例 |
|------|------------|------|
| 一般现在时 | do / does | **Do** you like coffee? |
| 一般过去时 | did | **Did** she go home? |
| 一般将来时 | will | I **will** call you. |
| 现在进行时 | am / is / are + V-ing | She **is reading** a book. |
| 过去进行时 | was / were + V-ing | They **were playing** football. |
| 现在完成时 | have / has + V-ed | I **have finished** my work. |
| 过去完成时 | had + V-ed | He **had left** before I arrived. |
| 将来完成时 | will have + V-ed | We **will have finished** by then. |

---

## 5. 示例句型表格

| 时态 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| 一般现在时 | I **do** my homework. | I **do not** do my homework. | **Do** you do your homework? |
| 一般过去时 | She **did** her homework. | She **did not** do her homework. | **Did** she do her homework? |
| 现在完成时 | I **have done** my homework. | I **have not** done my homework. | **Have** you done your homework? |
| 将来时 | I **will do** my homework. | I **will not** do my homework. | **Will** you do your homework? |

---

## 6. 常见错误提示
- **错误**：He do his homework.  
  **正确**：He **does** his homework.
- **错误**：She did not went home.  
  **正确**：She **did not go** home.
- **错误**：I have ate lunch.  
  **正确**：I **have eaten** lunch.

---

## 7. 记忆技巧
- **do/does/did** → 用于一般时态的疑问和否定。
- **have/has/had** → 用于完成时态。
- **will/shall** → 用于将来时态。
- **be动词** → 用于进行时态和被动语态。
