# 情态动词学习手册

## 1. 概念说明
**情态动词（Modal Verbs）** 是英语中用来表达 **能力、可能性、许可、义务、推测** 等语气的助动词。  
它们后面必须跟 **动词原形**，不能单独使用。

---

## 2. 常见情态动词及含义

| 情态动词 | 含义 | 示例 |
|----------|------|------|
| can      | 能够 | I **can** swim. |
| could    | 能够（过去式）/可能 | She **could** help you. |
| may      | 可能/允许 | You **may** enter. |
| might    | 可能（比may弱） | It **might** rain. |
| must     | 必须 | You **must** stop. |
| shall    | 将要（正式） | I **shall** return. |
| should   | 应该 | You **should** study. |
| will     | 将要 | I **will** call you. |
| would    | 将要（过去式）/假设 | He **would** go if he could. |

---

## 3. 用法分类

### **(1) 表示能力**
- I **can** speak English.
- She **could** run fast when she was young.

### **(2) 表示可能性**
- It **may** rain tomorrow.
- He **might** be at home.

### **(3) 表示许可**
- You **may** leave now.
- Students **can** use the library.

### **(4) 表示义务或必要**
- You **must** wear a seatbelt.
- We **should** respect others.

### **(5) 表示将来或意愿**
- I **will** help you.
- He **would** join us if invited.

---

## 4. 常见错误提示
- **错误**：He can swims.  
  **正确**：He **can swim**. （情态动词后接动词原形）
- **错误**：She must to go.  
  **正确**：She **must go**. （不能加“to”）

---

## 5. 示例表格（常用句型）

| 情态动词 | 肯定句 | 否定句 | 疑问句 |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|
| can      | I **can** drive. | I **cannot** drive. | **Can** you drive? |
| may      | You **may** enter. | You **may not** enter. | **May** I come in? |
| must     | You **must** stop. | You **must not** stop. | **Must** I stop? |
| will     | I **will** call you. | I **will not** call you. | **Will** you call me? |

---

## 6. 记忆技巧
- 情态动词后 **必须跟动词原形**。
- 没有 **-s** 形式（第三人称也不加 s）。
- 否定形式：直接加 **not**（如 cannot, will not）。
- 疑问句：情态动词提前到句首。

---

## 7. 常见缩写形式
- I will → **I'll**
- I would → **I'd**
- You will → **You'll**
- You would → **You'd**
- Cannot → **Can't**
- Will not → **Won't**
