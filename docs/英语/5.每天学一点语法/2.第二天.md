我们今天的第一主题是：**日期、时间 + 问候 & 见面场景**（包括陌生人见面、好友见面、外国人见面），并融入**be动词**（am/is/are/was/were）、**基本代词**（I/you/he/she/we/they）、**不定冠词**（a/an）、**介词**（at/on/in）、**简单情态动词**（can/may）等基础语法。

下面我会提供大量**中英双语句子**，每个句子后标注**重点/难点词汇**，并附上**KK音标**（美式为主，方便日常使用）+ 简单**词根词缀分析**。句子会覆盖食堂、餐厅、酒店、日常工作、workshop等场景的入门表达。

### 第一部分：日期 & 时间相关句子（重点Sight Words：today, tomorrow, yesterday, now, then, at, on, in, what, time, is, it）

1. **今天是2026年1月14日星期三。**  
   Today is Wednesday, January 14th, 2026.  
   **重点词汇**：today /ˈtəˈdeɪ/、is /ɪz/、Wednesday /ˈwɛnzdeɪ/、January /ˈdʒænjuˌɛri/  
   - today：to（向）+ day（天）→ “这一天”  
   - is：be动词第三人称单数现在时，源自古英语“is”，无明显词缀  
   - Wednesday：源自古英语“Wōdnesdæg”（沃登神之日），Woden（北欧神话主神）+ dæg（day）

2. **现在是什么时间？**  
   What time is it now?  
   **重点词汇**：what /wʌt/、time /taɪm/、it /ɪt/、now /naʊ/  
   - what：疑问代词，古英语“hwæt”，词根hw-（谁/什么）  
   - it：代词，指代时间/天气/情况，无词缀  
   - now：古英语“nū”，表示“当下”，无明显词缀

3. **我们下午两点半开会。**  
   We have a meeting at two thirty in the afternoon.  
   **重点词汇**：we /wi/、at /æt/、in /ɪn/、afternoon /ˌæftɚˈnun/  
   - we：人称代词复数第一人称，古英语“wē”  
   - at：介词，表示具体时间点，源自古英语“æt”  
   - in：介词，用于较大时间段（如in the morning/afternoon/evening）

4. **明天是我的生日！**  
   Tomorrow is my birthday!  
   **重点词汇**：tomorrow /təˈmɑroʊ/、my /maɪ/、birthday /ˈbɜːθdeɪ/  
   - tomorrow：to + morrow（古英语“morgen”早晨）→ “到明天”  
   - my：所有格代词，源自古英语“mīn”

5. **昨天我很忙。**  
   I was very busy yesterday.  
   **重点词汇**：was /wʌz/、very /ˈvɛri/、busy /ˈbɪzi/、yesterday /ˈjɛstɚdeɪ/  
   - was：be动词过去时第一/第三人称单数  
   - yesterday：yester（古英语“geostran”昨天）+ day

6. **会议定在周五上午十点。**  
   The meeting is on Friday at ten o'clock in the morning.  
   **重点词汇**：on /ɑn/、Friday /ˈfraɪdeɪ/、o'clock /əˈklɑk/  
   - on：介词，用于具体日期（如on Monday/on January 14th）

7. **现在几点了？已经很晚了。**  
   What time is it? It's already late.  
   **重点词汇**：already /ɔlˈrɛdi/、late /leɪt/  
   - already：all + ready → “全部准备好了”→ “已经”

8. **我们可以五点下班吗？**  
   Can we leave at five? / May we leave at five o'clock?  
   **重点词汇**：can /kæn/（能力/许可）、may /meɪ/（更正式的许可）  
   - can：情态动词，源自古英语“cunnan”（知道、能够）

9. **今天天气很好，对吧？**  
   It's nice today, isn't it?  
   **重点词汇**：nice /naɪs/、isn't /ˈɪznt/（is not缩写）  
   - isn't = is + not，否定形式

10. **我们下周一见。**  
    See you next Monday.  
    **重点词汇**：next /nɛkst/、see /si/  
    - next：古英语“niehsta”（最靠近的）

（继续扩展时间表达……我们再加20句左右）

11. **我通常七点起床。** Usually I get up at seven.  
    **重点**：usually /ˈjuʒuəli/（频度副词）

12. **会议从九点开始，到十二点结束。** The workshop starts at nine and ends at twelve.  
    **重点**：start /stɑrt/、end /ɛnd/、and /ænd/（连词）

13. **你几点到公司？** What time do you arrive at the office?  
    **重点**：do /du/（助动词，用于疑问/否定）

14. **我们十一点半吃午饭吧。** Let's have lunch at eleven thirty.  
    **重点**：let's = let us（建议句型）

15. **昨天我十一点才睡觉。** I went to bed at eleven last night.  
    **重点**：went /wɛnt/（go的过去式）、last /læst/

### 第二部分：见面问候场景（陌生人/好友/外国人）+ be动词 & 代词 & 不定冠词

16. **你好，我是新来的。**  
    Hello, I am new here.  
    **重点词汇**：hello /həˈloʊ/、I /aɪ/、am /æm/、new /nu/、here /hɪr/  
    - am：be动词第一人称单数现在时  
    - I：第一人称主格代词，大写是英语唯一要求

17. **很高兴认识你。**  
    Nice to meet you. / I'm glad to meet you.  
    **重点**：nice、to（不定式）、meet /mit/

18. **你是哪里人？**  
    Where are you from?  
    **重点**：where /wɛr/、are /ɑr/（be复数/第二人称）、from /frʌm/

19. **我来自中国。**  
    I am from China. / I'm Chinese.  
    **重点**：Chinese /tʃaɪˈniz/（形容词/名词）

20. **你叫什么名字？**  
    What's your name?  
    **重点**：your /jɔr/（所有格）、name /neɪm/

21. **我叫Entity，你呢？**  
    My name is Entity. And you?  
    **重点**：and（连词）

22. **好久不见！**  
    Long time no see! / It's been a long time!  
    **重点**：been /bɪn/（be的过去分词）

23. **你最近怎么样？**  
    How are you lately? / How have you been?  
    **重点**：how /haʊ/、lately /ˈleɪtli/、have /hæv/（助动词）

24. **我很好，你呢？**  
    I'm fine, and you? / I'm good, thanks.  
    **重点**：fine /faɪn/、good /ɡʊd/、thanks /θæŋks/

25. **这位是我的同事。**  
    This is my colleague. / He/She is my coworker.  
    **重点**：this /ðɪs/、colleague /ˈkɑliɡ/

### 第三部分：小场景对话练习（食堂/餐厅/酒店）

**食堂点餐对话**  
A: What would you like for lunch today?  
B: I'd like rice and chicken, please. Is it spicy?  
A: No, it's not spicy. It's very good.

**酒店前台**  
A: Hello, I have a reservation.  
B: What's your name, please?  
A: Entity233.  
B: Here is your key. Your room is on the 12th floor.

**Workshop破冰**  
Hi everyone! My name is Entity, and I'm from the marketing team.  
Nice to meet you all! Where are you from?

### 小作文（根据今天内容写的短文）

Today is Wednesday, January 14th, 2026. It is a sunny day and I am very happy. At eight o'clock in the morning, I arrive at the office. I say "Hello" to my colleagues. "Nice to meet you!" they answer.  

My name is Entity233. I am from China. I am new in this company. One coworker asks, "Where are you from?" I answer, "I am from China, and you?" We talk about time. "What time is the meeting?" I ask. "It is at ten o'clock," he says.  

At eleven thirty, we have lunch in the cafeteria. I say, "Can I sit here?" My friend answers, "Of course! Nice to see you!" We are very busy yesterday, but today is better. Tomorrow is Thursday. We will have a team build activity. I am excited!  

（这篇小作文用了今天重点的：be动词 am/is/are + at/on/in介词 + 代词 I/you/he + 情态can + 时间today/tomorrow/yesterday + 见面问候nice to meet/see + 连词and）
