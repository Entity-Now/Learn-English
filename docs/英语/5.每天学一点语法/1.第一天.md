我们会重点使用**Sight Words**（高频即时识词，如Dolch和Fry列表中最常见的那些），来构建简单、自然的句子。这些词包括：**I, you, he, she, it, we, they, is, are, am, was, were, be, can, could, may, might, will, would, should, must, have, has, do, does, in, on, at, to, from, with, for, of, the, a, an** 等。

我们今天的第一课主题是：**日常打招呼 + 时间/日期 + 自我介绍 + 陌生人/好友见面场景**（结合工作、餐厅、酒店等初步接触）。我会提供大量双语例句，每个句子后标注**重点/难点Sight Words**，并给出**音标**（使用国际音标IPA）和简单的**词根/词缀分析**（针对常见词）。

### 第一部分：日期和时间的基本表达（Date & Time）

1. Today is January 14th, 2026.  
   今天是2026年1月14日。  
   **重点Sight Words**: today, is, the  
   - today /təˈdeɪ/：to（向）+ day（天）→ “这一天”  
   - is /ɪz/：be动词第三人称单数现在时  
   - the /ðə/ 或 /ðiː/：定冠词，用于特指

2. What day is it today?  
   今天是星期几？  
   **重点Sight Words**: what, day, is, it, today  
   - what /wɒt/：疑问代词  
   - it /ɪt/：代词，指代时间/天气等

3. It is Monday today.  
   今天是星期一。  
   **重点Sight Words**: it, is, today

4. What time is it now?  
   现在几点了？  
   **重点Sight Words**: what, time, is, it, now  
   - now /naʊ/：副词，表示“现在”

5. It is nine o'clock in the morning.  
   现在是早上九点。  
   **重点Sight Words**: it, is, in, the  
   - o'clock /əˈklɒk/：of the clock的缩写

6. The meeting starts at 2:30 p.m.  
   会议下午2:30开始。  
   **重点Sight Words**: the, at  
   - start /stɑːrt/：动词，开始

7. I will see you tomorrow at 10 a.m.  
   我明天上午10点见你。  
   **重点Sight Words**: I, will, you, at  
   - tomorrow /təˈmɒrəʊ/：to + morrow（明天）

8. Yesterday was Sunday.  
   昨天是星期天。  
   **重点Sight Words**: was（be的过去式）

9. We have a workshop next week.  
   我们下周有个workshop。  
   **重点Sight Words**: we, have, next, week

10. It might rain this afternoon.  
    今天下午可能会下雨。  
    **重点Sight Words**: it, might, this  
    - might /maɪt/：情态动词，表示“可能”（较低可能性）

（继续扩展类似句子，共约40个时间日期句型……为了篇幅，这里精选展示，实际学习中可反复替换月份/时间。）

### 第二部分：Be动词核心句型（Be Verbs + Adjectives / Prepositions）

Be动词（am/is/are/was/were）是最重要的系动词，用来描述状态、身份、地点。

11. I am fine, thank you. And you?  
    我很好，谢谢。你呢？  
    **重点Sight Words**: I, am, you, and  
    - fine /faɪn/：形容词，好

12. She is from China.  
    她来自中国。  
    **重点Sight Words**: she, is, from

13. We are in the office now.  
    我们现在在办公室。  
    **重点Sight Words**: we, are, in, the, now

14. He was late yesterday.  
    他昨天迟到了。  
    **重点Sight Words**: he, was

15. They were happy at the team build.  
    他们在团建时很开心。  
    **重点Sight Words**: they, were, at

16. This is my friend, Mike.  
    这是我的朋友，Mike。  
    **重点Sight Words**: this, is, my

17. You are welcome to our hotel.  
    欢迎来我们的酒店。  
    **重点Sight Words**: you, are, to, our

18. It is cold in winter here.  
    这里冬天很冷。  
    **重点Sight Words**: it, is, in

19. I am hungry. Let's eat now.  
    我饿了。我们现在吃吧。  
    **重点Sight Words**: I, am, let's（let us缩写）

20. The food is good in this restaurant.  
    这家餐厅的食物很好。  
    **重点Sight Words**: the, is, in, this

### 第三部分：情态动词（Modal Verbs）日常使用

情态动词后直接接动词原形，非常常见于请求、建议、能力表达。

21. Can I help you?  
    我能帮你吗？（餐厅/酒店常用）  
    **重点Sight Words**: can, I, you, help

22. Could you please repeat that?  
    你能再说一遍吗？（陌生人见面）  
    **重点Sight Words**: could, you, please

23. May I have your name?  
    我可以知道你的名字吗？（正式初次见面）  
    **重点Sight Words**: may, I, your, have

24. You should try this dish.  
    你应该试试这道菜。（餐厅推荐）  
    **重点Sight Words**: you, should, this

25. We must finish the work today.  
    我们今天必须完成工作。  
    **重点Sight Words**: we, must, the, today

26. Might I join your team?  
    我可以加入你们团队吗？（workshop场景）  
    **重点Sight Words**: might, I

27. Would you like some water?  
    你要喝点水吗？（好友/客人）  
    **重点Sight Words**: would, you, like, some

28. I can speak English a little.  
    我会一点英语。  
    **重点Sight Words**: I, can, a, little

29. He could come to the meeting tomorrow.  
    他明天可能会来开会。  
    **重点Sight Words**: he, could, to, the, tomorrow

30. You must be tired after the long day.  
    你忙了一天一定很累吧。  
    **重点Sight Words**: you, must, be, after

### 第四部分：代词 + 介词 + 不定冠词/不定代词组合句子

代词（I/you/he/she/it/we/they/my/your/his/her/its/our/their）+ 介词（in/on/at/to/from/with/for/of）是日常句子的骨架。

31. This is a good restaurant for lunch.  
    这是一家吃午饭的好餐厅。  
    **重点Sight Words**: this, is, a, good, for

32. I have some friends in the company.  
    我在公司有一些朋友。  
    **重点Sight Words**: I, have, some, in, the

33. He is one of my best friends.  
    他是我最好的朋友之一。  
    **重点Sight Words**: he, is, one, of, my, best

34. We go to the cafeteria every day.  
    我们每天去食堂。  
    **重点Sight Words**: we, to, the, every, day

35. She sits with me at the table.  
    她和我坐一张桌子。  
    **重点Sight Words**: she, with, me, at, the

36. They are from different countries.  
    他们来自不同的国家。  
    **重点Sight Words**: they, are, from

37. This book is for you.  
    这本书是给你的。  
    **重点Sight Words**: this, is, for, you

38. Any questions?  
    有什么问题吗？（workshop结束常用）  
    **重点Sight Words**: any（不定代词）

39. Someone is calling you.  
    有人给你打电话。  
    **重点Sight Words**: some, one, is, you

40. Nothing is wrong.  
    没什么问题。  
    **重点Sight Words**: nothing（不定代词）

### 第五部分：连词 + 状语从句简单句（because, when, if, and, but）

41. I am late because the traffic is bad.  
    我迟到了因为交通很糟。  
    **重点Sight Words**: I, am, because, the, is

42. When you arrive, please call me.  
    你到了请给我打电话。  
    **重点Sight Words**: when, you, please, me

43. If it rains, we will stay inside.  
    如果下雨，我们就待在室内。  
    **重点Sight Words**: if, it, we, will

44. I like coffee and tea.  
    我喜欢咖啡和茶。  
    **重点Sight Words**: I, like, and

45. He is nice but very busy.  
    他人很好但很忙。  
    **重点Sight Words**: he, is, but, very

### 第六部分：场景综合对话练习（陌生人/好友/工作/餐厅/酒店）

**场景1：陌生人初次见面（外国人）**

A: Hello! Nice to meet you. I am Li Ming from China.  
   你好！很高兴认识你。我是中国来的李明。  

B: Hi! Nice to meet you too. My name is John. Where are you from?  
   嗨！也很高兴认识你。我叫John。你来自哪里？

**重点Sight Words**：hello, nice, to, meet, you, I, am, from, my, name, is, where, are

**场景2：好友见面 + 吃饭**

A: Hey! Long time no see! How are you?  
   嘿！好久不见！你好吗？

B: Great! And you? Let's have lunch together.  
   很好！你呢？我们一起吃午饭吧。

A: Sure! What do you want to eat?  
   当然！你想吃什么？

**场景3：酒店前台**

A: Good evening. Do you have a room for tonight?  
   晚上好。今晚有房间吗？

B: Yes, we do. Would you like a single or double room?  
   有。你要单人间还是双人间？

**重点Sight Words**：good, do, you, have, a, for, yes, we, would, like, or

**场景4：食堂/餐厅点餐**

A: Can I have the menu, please?  
   可以给我菜单吗？

B: Here you are. What would you like?  
   给你。你想要点什么？

A: I would like rice with chicken.  
   我想要鸡肉饭。

**场景5：Workshop/Team Build**

A: Hello everyone! We are starting the team build now.  
   大家好！我们现在开始团建。

B: Great! I am excited.  
   太好了！我很兴奋。

（以上场景可反复替换人名、食物、时间，练习替换。）

### 小作文（使用今天所学语法和单词）

Today is January 14th, 2026. It is Monday, and I am very happy. I can meet my new friend John at the restaurant at 12 o'clock. He is from America, and I am from China. We are both busy at work, but we must have lunch together because it is good to talk with friends.

When we arrive, I say: "Nice to meet you! How are you today?" He answers: "I am fine, thank you. And you?" We order food. I would like some rice, and he wants chicken. The food is good, and we are happy.

After lunch, we might go to a workshop. If it rains, we will stay inside. You should come too! It is fun when friends are together.

**小作文重点语法**：be动词（is/am/are）、情态动词（can/must/would/might/will）、介词（at/in/from/with）、连词（and/because/when/if）、不定冠词（a）、代词（I/he/we/you）、时间表达（today/at 12 o'clock）。
