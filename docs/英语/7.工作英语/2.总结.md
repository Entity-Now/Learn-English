# Summary of Commons

## 👋 **1. Basic Greeting 基本问候**

*   Hi, I’m here to get you.
*   I’m here to pick you up.
*   I’m XXX from XXX.
*   Nice to meet you.
*   Excuse me, are you XXX?
*   Just to confirm, are you XXX?

***

## ✈️ **2. After Meeting 在接上后**

*   How was your trip?
*   How was your flight?
*   Was everything okay during your journey?
*   Do you need a break or some water?

***

## 🚗 **3. Going to Hotel / Office 去酒店或公司**

*   Where would you like to go first — the hotel or our office?
*   Do you prefer to check in first?
*   Have you already booked a hotel?

**If NOT booked:**

*   No problem, I can help you find a hotel.
*   I can help you make a reservation.

**If booked:**

*   Great, I’ll take you there.
*   Perfect, let’s go to your hotel now.

***

## 🏨 **4. Hotel Preferences 酒店偏好**

*   What type of hotel do you prefer?
*   Do you prefer something close to the office or the city center?
*   Do you have a price range in mind?

***

## 🗣 **5. Conversation Support 沟通辅助**

*   Could you speak a little more slowly?
*   Sorry, could you repeat that more slowly?
*   Please let me know if I misunderstand anything.

***

## 😀 **6. Friendly Small Talk 轻松聊天**

*   Is this your first time in Changshu?
*   Our company is about 20 minutes from here.
*   If you need anything, just let me know.
*   Is the temperature comfortable for you?

***

## 📌 **7. Safe Polite Phrases 安全礼貌用语**

*   Please feel free to ask me anything.
*   Let me know if you need help.
*   I’m happy to assist.
*   Would you like to stop for coffee or water?

# 🏢 **Office Tour – Useful English Sentences（办公室参观常用句）**

## ⭐ 1. 我的工位 / 你的工位（Workstations）

*   **My desk is over there.**
*   **Your desk is on this side.**
*   **You will be sitting over here.**
*   **Let me show you your seat.**
*   **If you need anything at your desk, just let me know.**

***

## ⭐ 2. 洗手间（Restroom）

*   **The restroom is ahead, then turn left.**
*   **The restroom is on the right side of the hallway.**
*   **There are restrooms on each floor.**
*   **If you need the restroom later, feel free to ask me.**

***

## ⭐ 3. 楼梯、通道、电梯（Stairs, Hallways, Elevators）

*   **There are three ways to go upstairs.**
*   **You can use the stairs or the elevator.**
*   **This hallway leads to the meeting rooms.**
*   **The elevator is at the end of this corridor.**

***

## ⭐ 4. 食堂 / 餐厅（Cafeteria / Canteen）

*   **That is our cafeteria.**
*   **You need to enter through the door on the right.**
*   **Lunch time starts at 11:30.**
*   **We usually go to lunch around 11:30.**
*   **You can pay with cash, card, or mobile.**

***

## ⭐ 5. 会议室（Meeting Rooms）

*   **This is our main meeting room.**
*   **We usually use this room for team meetings.**
*   **You can book a meeting room online.**
*   **The meeting rooms are numbered from 101 to 108.**

***

## ⭐ 6. 休息区（Break Area）

*   **This is the break area.**
*   **Here we have a fridge, microwave, coffee machine, and hot water dispenser.**
*   **Feel free to use any of the appliances.**
*   **Snacks are usually placed on this table.**
*   **You can sit here anytime if you want to rest.**

***

## ⭐ 7. 茶水间（Pantry）

*   **This is our pantry.**
*   **You can make coffee or tea here.**
*   **The cups are in the cabinet.**
*   **Please clean the table after use.**

***

## ⭐ 8. 打印区（Printing Area）

*   **This is the printing and copying area.**
*   **You can print, copy, or scan documents here.**
*   **If the printer is out of paper, let me know.**
*   **Your access card will unlock the printer.**

***

## ⭐ 9. 安全与门禁（Access & Safety）

*   **You need your badge to enter this area.**
*   **Please always keep your badge with you.**
*   **Emergency exits are on both sides of the floor.**
*   **We have fire drills twice a year.**

***

## ⭐ 10. 其他常用补充句（General Helper Phrases）

*   **If you want to walk around the office later, feel free.**
*   **Let me know if you need help finding anything.**
*   **You will get used to the layout very quickly.**
*   **I can show you around anytime.**
