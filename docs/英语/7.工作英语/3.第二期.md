## 📖 词汇详解 (Vocabulary Details)

### 01. robust
* **音标/词性**: `/rəʊˈbʌst/` · `adj.`
* **核心释义**: 强健的、体魄结实的；（系统、架构等）稳定可靠的、健壮的
> **常用搭配 (Collocations)**
> * **robust health** 强健的体魄
> * **robust system** 稳定可靠的系统
> * **robust architecture** 健壮的系统架构
> 
> **例句 (Example Sentences)**
> * He keeps **robust health** by regular exercise. 
>   *他通过规律锻炼保持强健的体魄。*
> * This software has a **robust system** to resist unexpected errors.
>   *这款软件搭载稳定可靠的系统，可抵御突发故障。*

> **常用搭配 (Collocations)**
> * **regular exercise** 规律锻炼
> * **regular physical check** 常规体检
> * **regular price** 原价、常规售价
> * **regular size** 标准尺寸 / 标准大小
> 
> **例句 (Example Sentences)**
> * **Regular exercise** is good for your physical health.
>   *规律的锻炼有益于你的身体健康。*
> * This is the **regular menu** of the restaurant.
>   *这是餐厅的正价菜单。*
