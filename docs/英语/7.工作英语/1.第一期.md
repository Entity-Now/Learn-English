# 英语沟通模板 - 办公室与商务场景（完整版）

## 一、办公室环境介绍 (Office Environment Introduction)

### 1. 基本设施介绍

#### 常用例句

- "This is our main office area."

- "That's the conference room where we hold meetings."

- "This is the reception desk at the entrance."

- "Over there is the copy room with printers and scanners."

- "This area is our open workspace for team collaboration."

- "That's the quiet room for focused work or private calls."

- "This is the server room where we keep all our IT equipment."

- "Over there is the supply closet with stationery and office supplies."

- "这里是 xxxx 的" → "This is the [specific area]."

- "那里是厕所" → "That's the restroom over there."

#### 难点词汇

|词汇|音标|解释|
|---|---|---|
|reception|/rɪˈsepʃn/|n. 接待处|
|conference|/ˈkɒnfərəns/|n. 会议|
|collaboration|/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃn/|n. 合作，协作|
|stationery|/ˈsteɪʃənri/|n. 文具|
|equipment|/ɪˈkwɪpmənt/|n. 设备，装备|
#### 语法总结

- **指示代词用法**：This is.../ That's... 用于介绍近处和远处的事物

- **定语从句**：where we hold meetings 修饰 conference room

- **介词短语**：at the entrance, with printers and scanners 用于补充说明
